The Hunter Odyssey

A 2D JRPG made from scratch with SDL and C++

If the video doesn't work, click HERE

About the videogame

Hunter Odyssey is a game where you find yourself submerged in a world of fantasy, with dangerous enemies and unique puzzles. Play as multiple characters to achieve your goal in this brand new game!
The Hunter Odyssey is a RPG turn-based video game. You are a bounty hunter who has heard rumors about the prince from the most important region of the country, who is supposedly kidnapped by a majestuous monster. So, you decide to visit that famous place, with the objective of discovering all the secrets around this case.

About the creation of the videogame

This video game was created together with six other classmates. When this videogame was made, I already had some notions of C++ and I think the final result of the videogame is really good.

This project also helped me to learn how to work with EASTL, which is almost identical to STL. So I could get an idea of how STL works, as I had not worked with it in any project until then.

Third Party Libraries

To create this videogame, the following softwares/libraries were used:

  • EASTL
  • PhysFS
  • Pugi XML
  • SDL
  • SDL Image
  • SDL Mixer
  • Tiled
