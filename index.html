About me

My name is Lucas Pérez and I'm a game developer from Barcelona. I really like to develop video games and keep improving my C++ skills. My main goal is to work in the game industry as a gameplay programmer.

At this moment I'm studying a bachelor degree in Video Game Design and Development in CITM university. We mainly learn how to program with C++ but we also learn proper techniques about game design and art.

In this portfolio you can see all the projects I've done and you can also my github.
